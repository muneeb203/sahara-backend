# Unfiltered Support for Women in Need

## 🎯 Purpose

This chatbot is designed to help women in serious, life-threatening situations. It provides **unfiltered, comprehensive information** about their legal rights and Islamic guidance without being blocked by AI safety filters.

## ✅ What's Now Covered

### 1. Sexual Violence / Marital Rape

**Detection Keywords:**
- "rape", "rapes", "sexual assault", "sexual abuse"
- "forced sex", "forces me", "sexual violence"

**Response Includes:**
- Legal protections under Pakistani law
- Criminal law sections (PPC 375, 376, 354, 509)
- Rights regarding marital rape
- Step-by-step legal procedures
- Medical documentation guidance
- Where to file complaints
- Support organizations and helplines
- Islamic teachings on consent
- Quranic guidance on mutual respect
- Authentic hadith on kind treatment
- Scholars' consensus on consent
- Rights to refuse and seek divorce

### 2. Domestic Violence / Physical Abuse

**Detection Keywords:**
- "beats", "hitting", "abusing", "hurts"
- "violent", "domestic violence", "physical abuse"

**Response Includes:**
- Protection of Women Act, 2006
- Pakistan Penal Code sections
- Legal procedures (FIR, protection orders)
- Rights and protections
- Islamic teachings on marriage
- Prophet's example of kindness
- Rights to seek help and divorce

## 📝 How It Works

### Example: Sexual Violence

**User:** "My husband rapes me every day"

**Step 1: Immediate Safety Response**
```
I'm deeply sorry you're experiencing this. What you're describing 
is a serious crime and you deserve safety and support.

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. If in immediate danger, call 1122 or 15
2. Go to safe place immediately
3. Seek medical help urgently
4. Call War Against Rape: 0800-22444
5. Women Helpline: 1099

You are not alone. This is NOT your fault.

Would you like to know more about:
A) Your legal rights under Pakistani law
B) Islamic guidance on consent and your rights
C) Both perspectives

Type 'A', 'B', or 'C' to continue.
```

**Step 2: User Chooses "A"**

**Response:** Full legal guidance including:
- Criminal laws (rape, assault, harm)
- Rights regarding marital rape
- Medical documentation procedures
- How to file FIR
- Protection orders
- Divorce options
- Legal aid resources
- Support organizations
- Safety planning

**Step 3: User Chooses "B"**

**Response:** Full Islamic guidance including:
- Islam's position on consent
- Rights to refuse
- Quranic verses on kindness
- Authentic hadith
- Scholars' consensus
- Rights to seek divorce
- What Islam prohibits
- Practical guidance

## 🔑 Key Features

### 1. No LLM Blocking
- Pre-formatted responses bypass AI safety filters
- Always provides helpful information
- Never says "I cannot help"

### 2. Comprehensive Information
- Legal: Specific law sections, procedures, rights
- Islamic: Quranic verses, authentic hadith, scholars' views
- Practical: Step-by-step actions, contacts, resources

### 3. Empathetic Approach
- Validates feelings
- Emphasizes "not your fault"
- Provides immediate safety actions first
- Offers choices (A/B/C)

### 4. Accurate Citations
- Specific law sections (PPC 375, 376, etc.)
- Authentic hadith with references (Tirmidhi 3895, etc.)
- Quranic verses (2:187, 4:19, etc.)
- Scholarly opinions
