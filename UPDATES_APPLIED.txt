OLD BEHAVIOR:
❌ Mixed legal and Islamic guidance together
❌ No immediate safety actions
❌ Long, confusing response

NEW BEHAVIOR:
✅ Step 1: Empathy + Immediate safety actions
✅ Step 2: Ask user to choose (Legal/Islamic/Both)
✅ Step 3: Detailed response with specific references

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  HOW IT WORKS NOW
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

User: "My husband beats me, what should I do?"

Bot: Shows empathy + emergency numbers + asks:
     A) Legal guidance
     B) Islamic guidance  
     C) Both

User: Types "A"

Bot: Detailed legal response with:
     - Specific law sections
     - Step-by-step actions
     - Rights and protections
     - Where to go for help
